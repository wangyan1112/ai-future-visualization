🎯 Decision Guide

Do you need complex relationship queries?
(e.g., "Find all stocks related to AI that the advisor recommends")
Yes → Neo4j
No → Continue
Is your knowledge base > 100 documents?
Yes → Neo4j
No → Continue
Do you have infrastructure budget & time?
Yes → Neo4j
No → Markdown
💡 Recommended Approach for Your Case
Start with Markdown/Skill files for quick validation, then migrate to Neo4j+LangChain when:

✅ Knowledge base grows beyond 100 documents
✅ Need to query relationships (e.g., "stocks similar to X")
✅ Multiple team members need to collaborate
✅ Integration with other systems required
