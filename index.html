The Common Mistakes (Loss)

📢Tip-Based Trading

Buys based on news headlines, forum rumors, or "hot tips" without understanding the underlying business.

⚡Impatience & Speculation

Expects quick riches (weeks/days). Gets bored if a stock doesn't move immediately. Chases highs (FOMO) and cuts lows (Panic).

🎲Over-Diversification / Gambling

Buys 20+ stocks with small amounts because "one might pop," treating the market like a lottery ticket.

🐑Herd Mentality

Buys when everyone is celebrating (Top), sells when everyone is crying (Bottom). Reacts to price, not value.

📉Emotional Volatility

Let's fear and greed dictate actions. Sells winners too early (to lock small profit) and holds losers too long (hoping for rebound).

🚀 Actionable Steps to Shift Your Mindset

To move from the right column to the left, start doing these today:

  • Stop checking prices daily. Focus on business performance (earnings, growth), not ticker movement.
  • Write down your thesis. Before buying, write 3 reasons WHY. If the price drops but reasons hold, buy more.
  • Extend your timeline. Don't buy a stock you wouldn't be happy holding for 3 years if the market closed.
  • Study the business, not the chart. Spend 90% of your time researching the company/industry, 10% on execution.
